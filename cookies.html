Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function and provide information about how the site is used.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for essential functionality only. As a corporate website, our cookie usage is minimal.

Strictly Necessary Cookies

Session cookie (browsing session), cookie preference cookie (12 months), and CSRF token. These are essential and cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

Anonymous usage data collected only with your explicit consent. No personal identification. No sharing with advertising networks.

We do not use advertising or targeting cookies, social media tracking cookies, or fingerprinting.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We host all resources on our own infrastructure. No embedded third-party content that sets cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

A consent banner allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings.

5. Changes

Updated periodically at xenomere.com/cookies.
