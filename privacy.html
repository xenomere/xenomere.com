Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who We Are

Xenomere OU ("Xenomere," "we," "us," or "our") is an Estonian-registered private limited company (registry code 17507482) with its registered office at Narva mnt 5, 10117 Tallinn, Estonia.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal data when you visit xenomere.com or engage with us as a business partner, supplier, or prospective client. For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at support@xenomere.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate as a B2B sourcing company.

Information you provide directly: Name, organization, job title, email address, phone number, and details about your sourcing requirements (compound names, CAS numbers, specifications).

Information collected automatically: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited via standard server logs, and cookie data as described in our Cookie Policy.

We do NOT collect: Payment card details (we do not process online payments on this site), sensitive personal data (health information, government IDs, biometrics), or consumer data (we do not sell to consumers).

3. How We Use Your Data

We use personal data exclusively for B2B business purposes: responding to sourcing inquiries, communicating about orders and logistics, managing supplier and client relationships, improving our website and services, and complying with legal obligations (Estonian Commercial Code, EU regulations). We do not use personal data for automated decision-making or profiling.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the EU GDPR, our legal bases are: contractual necessity, legitimate interest, legal obligation, and consent (where you have explicitly agreed, e.g., cookie preferences).

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share data only as necessary with logistics partners, analytical testing laboratories, IT service providers (all servers within the EU/EEA), and regulatory authorities when required by law. We do NOT sell, rent, or trade personal data to any third party for marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

Inquiry data: 3 years from last contact. Transactional data: 7 years (Estonian accounting requirements). Website analytics: 26 months.

7. Data Security

We implement SSL/TLS encryption, access controls, regular security assessments, and EU-based server infrastructure.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to processing, data portability, withdraw consent, and lodge a complaint. Email support@xenomere.com. Supervisory authority: Estonian Data Protection Inspectorate (www.aki.ee).

9. International Transfers

Data is stored within the EU. Where transfers outside the EU/EEA are necessary, Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards apply.

10. Changes to This Policy

Updates posted at xenomere.com/privacy. Material changes communicated to active partners via email.
