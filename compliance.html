Compliance

Regulatory-aligned. Documented. Transparent.

As an EU-registered entity, Xenomere OU operates within the full framework of European Union regulatory requirements. Our sourcing practices, supplier relationships, and operational procedures are designed for compliance, traceability, and accountability.

Compliance Framework

EU

EU Corporate Governance

Registered in Estonia under the Estonian Commercial Code. Annual reporting, auditable records, transparent ownership structure. Publicly verifiable via the Estonian Business Register.

GMP

GMP Supply Chain

We partner exclusively with manufacturers holding current Good Manufacturing Practice certifications recognised by EU competent authorities. Supplier qualifications are verified before engagement.

DOC

Documentation Standards

Every transaction is accompanied by Certificates of Analysis, Safety Data Sheets, and full chain-of-custody records. Documentation maintained for audit and regulatory review.

AML

Financial Compliance

All transactions conducted through regulated EU financial institutions via SEPA bank transfer. Full transaction records maintained for audit and AML compliance.

EXP

Export Control

Compliance with EU dual-use and controlled substance export regulations. All cross-border shipments reviewed for licensing requirements before dispatch.

GDPR

Data Protection

GDPR-compliant handling of all partner and transaction data. EU-based server infrastructure. Personal data never shared with unauthorised third parties.

Quality Statement: Xenomere OU maintains a strict independent analytical verification policy. Every sourced compound is tested by an accredited third-party laboratory before release. We reject any material that does not meet the agreed specification. This is non-negotiable.
