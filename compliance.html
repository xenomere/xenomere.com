Compliance

Regulatory-Aligned. Documented. Transparent.

As an EU-registered entity, Xenomere OU operates within the full framework of European Union regulatory requirements.

Compliance Framework

🇪🇺

EU Corporate Governance

Registered in Estonia under the Estonian Commercial Code. Annual reporting, auditable records, transparent ownership structure.

🏭

GMP Supply Chain

We partner exclusively with manufacturers holding current GMP certifications recognized by EU competent authorities.

📄

Documentation Standards

Every transaction accompanied by COA, SDS, and full chain-of-custody records.

🏦

Anti-Money Laundering Compliance

All transactions conducted exclusively through regulated EU financial institutions (SEPA/SWIFT). No cash, no cryptocurrency, no unregulated payment channels.

📦

Export Control

We comply with EU dual-use and controlled substance export regulations. All cross-border shipments reviewed for licensing requirements.

🔒

Data Protection

GDPR-compliant handling of all partner and transaction data. Servers within the EU.

Quality Statement: Xenomere OU maintains a strict independent analytical verification policy. Every sourced compound is tested by an accredited third-party laboratory before release to our partners. We reject any material that does not meet the agreed specification. This is non-negotiable.
