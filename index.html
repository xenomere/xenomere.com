Our Services

Three integrated capabilities for research organisations across the European Union.

Synthesis

Custom Synthesis Sourcing

We connect R&D teams with specialised CDMOs for custom chemical reagent synthesis, small molecule APIs, and complex organic compounds — from milligram to kilogram scale — across our vetted network of GMP-certified manufacturers.

QC

Quality-Verified Supply

Every sourced compound undergoes independent third-party analytical verification — HPLC, mass spectrometry, NMR — before release. We work exclusively with accredited laboratories and provide full documentation with every delivery.

Reg

Regulatory-Aligned Operations

All sourcing activities operate within EU regulatory frameworks. Full traceability from manufacturer to delivery. Documentation packages include Certificates of Analysis, Safety Data Sheets, and chain-of-custody records.

Industries We Serve

Xenomere supplies research institutions, biotechnology companies, pharmaceutical development teams, and industrial laboratories across the European Union.

Biotechnology R&D

Supporting early-stage and established biotech companies with reliable access to high-purity chemical reagents, biologics, and small molecule compounds for discovery and preclinical research.

Academic & Institutional Research

Providing university laboratories, research institutes, and public-sector research organisations with documented, analytically verified compounds for fundamental and applied research programmes.

Industrial Testing & Quality Control

Supplying analytical reference standards, pharmaceutical intermediates, and specialty compounds to industrial QC laboratories and contract testing organisations.
