What We Do

Three pillars of B2B chemical sourcing for research organizations across the European Union.

🧬

Custom Synthesis Sourcing

We connect R&D teams with specialized CDMOs for custom peptide synthesis, small molecule APIs, and complex organic compounds from milligram to kilogram scale.

🔍

Quality-Verified Supply

Every sourced compound undergoes independent third-party analytical verification (HPLC, MS, NMR) before delivery to our partners. We never ship unverified material.

📋

Regulatory-Aligned Operations

All sourcing activities are conducted within EU regulatory frameworks with full traceability, documentation, and compliance at every step.

Who We Work With

Xenomere serves research institutions, biotechnology startups, pharmaceutical development teams, and industrial laboratories across the European Union.

Biotechnology R&D

Supporting early-stage and established biotech companies with reliable access to high-purity peptides, biologics, and small molecule compounds for discovery and preclinical research.

Academic & Institutional Research

Providing university laboratories, research institutes, and public-sector research organizations with documented, analytically verified compounds for fundamental and applied research.

Industrial Testing & QC

Supplying analytical reference standards, intermediates, and specialty compounds to industrial quality control and testing laboratories across the EU.
