Our Process

Every sourcing engagement follows a rigorous six-step process.

STEP 1

Partner Requirement Analysis

We define compound specifications, purity requirements, quantity, timeline, and regulatory parameters.

STEP 2

Manufacturer Identification

We identify qualified GMP-certified manufacturers from our vetted network.

STEP 3

Supplier Qualification

We verify certifications, audit documentation, and confirm batch traceability.

STEP 4

Order Management

We manage procurement, documentation (COA, SDS), and logistics from manufacturer to your facility.

STEP 5

Independent Analytical Verification

Every delivery undergoes third-party HPLC, MS, or NMR analysis. We never ship unverified material.

STEP 6

Ongoing Partnership

We maintain relationships with multiple manufacturers per compound class for supply chain resilience.
